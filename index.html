Real satellite data

Physical risk variables use ISRO Bhuvan flood susceptibility layers, MODIS 8-day Land Surface Temperature composites (calibrated ±0.5K), and Sentinel-2 NDWI indices — validated against NRSC flood atlas 1998–2022 covering 25 years of events.

PCA-derived weights

Layer weights (0.45 / 0.30 / 0.25) are not hand-picked. Derived from Principal Component Analysis on a 25×12 normalised ward matrix using scikit-learn. PC1 explains 82.6% of variance — statistically confirming a composite index is valid.

Confidence-adjusted scoring

Every variable carries a confidence score (data quality × source reliability × resolution fit). Low-confidence variables (e.g. IRDAI insurance proxy at 0.45) contribute proportionally less. Mean confidence 0.709 — clearly disclosed, not hidden.

Monte Carlo uncertainty

1,000 simulations per ward with Gaussian noise scaled to data confidence. Average CI width 3–4 CRCS points. Borderline wards (CI crossing a band boundary) are flagged for manual review — the model does not pretend to precision it doesn't have.
